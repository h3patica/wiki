hdb, or hepatica database, is my own small personal wiki for storing information. It’s based off of a list of linked files, where each page’s “children” and “parents” are listed in their filenames, and are navigable at the top of the page. Most of the things you’ll find here involve either personal journal-esque writing, or articles that are more intended to be read by others, on topics generally relating to computer science or politics.

The logo for hdb is the alchemical symbol for black sulfur, also known as a Leviathan Cross or Satan’s Cross. I think the meaning of the symbol is neat, but really I only use it because it acts as a pretty monogram of hdb (‘db’ at the bottom, ‘H’ along the central line).
